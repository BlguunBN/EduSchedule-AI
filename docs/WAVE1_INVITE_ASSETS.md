Send this when their invite is live (Wave 1, Day 0).

> **Your beta invite is ready!**
>
> Sign in here: **[YOUR_APP_URL]/login**
> Use your university Microsoft/Outlook account — same one you use for email.
>
> **3 things to do first:**
>
> 1. **Timetable** (Dashboard → Timetable)
>    Add your classes — type them in or upload a CSV/ICS from the student system.
>
> 2. **Email Scan** (Dashboard → Email)
>    Tap "Scan inbox" — it reads your academic emails and flags any schedule changes.
>    Nothing is sent out; it only reads.
>
> 3. **Reminders** (Dashboard → Reminders)
>    Check what's been auto-generated from your schedule. You can edit or dismiss any of them.
>
> That's it. If anything breaks or feels off, message me directly — that's literally why I'm testing it with you first.
>
> Known quirk: login redirects to Microsoft, takes ~10 seconds, then lands on the dashboard. That's normal.

## Known Caveats to Mention (if asked)

Be upfront about these so users don't feel surprised:

| Caveat | What to say |
|--------|-------------|
| Email scan reads Outlook only | "It only works with your university Microsoft/Outlook account — not Gmail." |
| Changes need approval | "It flags email changes for you to approve; nothing auto-updates without you seeing it." |
| Email digest reminders not wired | "Reminder emails aren't sent yet — reminders show up in the app for now." |
| Calendar write-back is local | "It tracks changes locally; it doesn't push back to your Outlook Calendar yet." |
